MUSIC DESIGNER

Music solutions for media and design

Who we are?

We are french, both musicians and sound engineer specialized in musical creation. We provide musical solutions for brands and media agencies. With our expertise in artistic direction. We are particularly sensitive to the aesthetic of sound. Just like the visual object, we integrate the sound into the absolute design field in order to supply an unsuspecting emotional dimension to your brand identity.

Our services

We take place in several creation area: The original composition, post production and music catalogue management through our web application "mood.io ©".

Original composition

We closely compose an original music with you in order to sustain at best your commercial project.

Post-production

Post-production of sound is the key of success factor which enable to further strengthen the storytelling, the clarity of message and the aesthetic of the film.

Mood.io ©

Mood.io is a web application which enable composers to provide their music sort by mood/ambiance.
